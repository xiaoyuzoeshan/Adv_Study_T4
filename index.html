The stakes

Lease your frequencies from someone else, and if they go under, your satellite becomes an illegal resident of space — broadcasting on a slot it no longer has the right to use.

Spectrum rights are awarded first-come, first-served. The earlier you file, the stronger your position. A single error can send you to the back of a years-long queue — or cancel your claim entirely.

01

Too costly for the small

Startups and small operators rarely have the budget or the in-house specialists to prepare a filing. So they lease, delay, or take on risk they don't fully understand.

02

Too slow for the large

Big operators file in bulk. Each filing means weeks of expert engineering time. The work is repetitive, deadline-bound, and a constant drain on scarce specialists.

03

Too easy to get wrong

A mismatched parameter, a missed window, an incomplete coordination — any one can void a filing, lose a priority date, or trigger interference disputes that take years to settle.
